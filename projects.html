Project 1: Resume

The Resume project showcases my proficiency in understanding basic HTML and CSS. This project involved creating a personal resume designed to present my professional and educational background in a clean, organized, and visually appealing manner. It serves as a digital resume to potential employers, highlighting my skills, achievements and experiences effectively.

Key features:

  • Design: The layout adjusts seamlessly to different screen sizes, ensuring an optimal viewing experience on both desktop and mobile devices.
  • Navigation Menu: A user-friendly navigation bar allows visitors to easily access different sections of the resume, such as Summary, Education, Experience, and Skills.
  • Professional Layout: The design emphasizes clarity and professionalism, with a structured layout that highlights my academic background and work experience.
  • Interactive Elements: Includes links and buttons for enhanced user interaction, such as a “Get in touch” button for contact purposes.
